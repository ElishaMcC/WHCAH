Game Description

This project implements a web-based match 3 puzzle game similar to Chuzzle, where the goal is to match 3 or more gems in a 6x6 grid. The player initially has 1 minute, but can gain small amounts of time by making matches and having a high combo. During the game, there is a small chance (that increases as the player has a higher score) for a gem on screen to become "locked" where the column and row that the "locked" gem is in can no longer be moved until a match is made adjacent to said gem.
